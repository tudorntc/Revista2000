1900-1910

The dawn of the modern age. First powered flight by Wright Brothers (1903), Ford Model T production begins (1908), and the foundation of modern industrial society.

1900 - 1910

1910-1920

World War I accelerates technology. First transcontinental telephone call (1915), radio broadcasting experiments, and mass production techniques revolutionize manufacturing.

1910 - 1920

1920-1930

The Roaring Twenties bring radio broadcasting to homes, first television demonstrations, and the beginning of consumer electronics. Jazz Age meets technology.

1920 - 1930

1930-1940

Despite economic hardship, innovation continues. First electronic computers concepts, radar development, and television broadcasts begin in major cities.

1930 - 1940

1940-1950

World War II drives massive technological advancement. ENIAC computer (1946), transistor invention (1947), and the foundation of modern computing.

1940 - 1950

1950-1960

Post-war boom brings consumer electronics. First computers for business, television becomes mainstream, and the space race begins with Sputnik (1957).

1950 - 1960

1960-1970

The Space Age and counterculture. Moon landing (1969), integrated circuits, early computer networks, and the birth of Silicon Valley innovation.

1960 - 1970

1970-1980

Personal computing revolution begins. Apple II (1977), Microsoft founded (1975), and the first steps toward putting computers in every home.

1970 - 1980

1980-1990

PC revolution explodes. IBM PC (1981), Apple Macintosh (1984), and the internet's predecessor ARPANET connects universities worldwide.

1980 - 1990

1990-2000

The World Wide Web transforms everything. First websites (1991), Netscape browser, Amazon and Google founded, and the dot-com boom begins.

1990 - 2000

2000-2010

Social media emerges. Facebook (2004), YouTube (2005), iPhone (2007), and the transformation from desktop to mobile computing begins.

2000 - 2010

2010-2020

Mobile-first world. Instagram, WhatsApp, cloud computing dominance, and smartphones become more powerful than desktop computers.

2010 - 2020

2020-2025

AI revolution begins. ChatGPT transforms how we work, remote work becomes standard, and artificial intelligence starts reshaping every industry.

2020 - 2025

The Future

What's next? Quantum computing, advanced AI, space colonization, and technologies we can't yet imagine. The journey through time continues...
